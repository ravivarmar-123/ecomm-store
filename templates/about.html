We buy it for you #StayAtHomeSmoothQuarantine

Smoothquarentine website is launched for the purpose of providing service to people who are stuck at home during lockdown in the Toronto area. We aspire to delivery basic groceries to customers in a safe and secure way in the time of a health crisis.

Vision

Every person should have a safe and comfortable quarantine


Mission

We deliver to our customers 24 hours a day and 7 days a week during lockdown and quarantine, effectively reducing the need for people to venture outside during a pandemic.
